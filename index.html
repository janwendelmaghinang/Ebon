EBON

Electronic Bird Observation and Neutralization

EBON

The EBON system is an innovative tool designed to benefit rice farmers by offering real-time bird monitoring and data collection. It helps protect rice crops from avian pests and provides valuable insights into bird behavior to aid in sustainable farming practices. EBON aims to support rice farmers in minimizing crop damage while promoting responsible bird observation.
